My Neighbor

TOTORO

1988 | Kodomo | 1h26min | Anime | Language: Japanese

Written by : Hayao Miyazaki

Cast : Noriko Hidaka, Chika Sakamoto, Shigesato Itoi

Genre : Fantasy and Family Animation

Sisters Satsuki and Mei move to the countryside to be closer to the hospital where their mother is hospitalized. There meet the Totoro, adorable, mystical and cheerful creatures that can only be seen by children. With them, the two sisters will live magical adventures in the countryside.
