A Gentle World by Hayao Miyazaki

Step into a peaceful universe inspired by nature, childhood, and magical storytelling from Studio Ghibli.

Hayao Miyazaki (宮崎 駿)

Hayao Miyazaki is a legendary Japanese animator, director, and co-founder of Studio Ghibli. His films are known for their emotional depth, hand-drawn beauty, and deep respect for nature.

Through calm storytelling and strong characters, Miyazaki teaches us about peace, kindness, love, and the beauty of simple life.

Famous Ghibli Works

My Neighbor Totoro

Warm story of childhood, family, and nature.

Spirited Away

A magical journey of growth, courage, and kindness.

Princess Mononoke

A powerful tale about nature and human conflict.

Howl’s Moving Castle

A romantic fantasy about love and self-acceptance.
