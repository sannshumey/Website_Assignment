PONYO

2008 | Kodomo | 1h43min | Anime | Language: Japanese

Written by : Hayao Miyazaki

Cast : Tomoko Yamaguchi, Kazushige Nagashima, Yuki Amami

Genre : Animated fantasy, Adventure, and Children's/family

The boy Sousuke finds a goldfish trapped in a bottle and decides to frees it, not knowing that it is the sea goddess Ponyo.Daughter of a powerful wizard,she is moved by the boy's attitude and uses her father's magic to transform into a human.This way, he believes he can strengthen his friendship with Sousuke. However,the substance in his magic potion could put the village where the boy lives at risk.
