Howl's Moving Castle

2004 | Kodomo | 1h59min | Anime | Language: Japanese

Written by : Hayao Miyazaki

Cast : Takuya Kimura, Emily Mortimer, Tatsuya Gashuin

Genre : Advanture, Time Travel, Fantasy Magic

Sophie Hatter, an unconfident young milliner, is cursed by the jealous Witch of the Waste into becoming a 90-year-old woman. Unable to tell anyone about the spell, she flees her home and finds work as a cleaning lady in the eccentric, walking castle of Wizard Howl. Inside, she befriends Howl's fire demon Calcifer and his young apprentice Markl. As a devastating war rages, Howl is pressured to fight for the king, but he is a pacifist who secretly uses his magic to interfere in the conflict, transforming into a monstrous bird-like creature and risking his humanity. Sophie's compassion and determination help Howl to confront his former teacher, Madame Suliman, end the war, and ultimately break both her curse and the contract between Howl and Calcifer.
